Some problems I care about:
  • Democratic decay
    • The continued success of democracy requires both high standards of living and growth
    • Technology is the primary force for both
    • We need to clear barriers to domestic building and let the market work in areas like housing, education, energy, and manufacturing
    • At the same time, government intervention sometimes makes sense, e.g. healthcare
  • Artificial intelligence
    • AI is poised to massively boost both standards of living and growth
    • The most likely risk is that AI will boost growth but not standards of living
    • The worst risk is that AI will wipe out humanity, which is less likely but obviously worth taking seriously
  • Raising the ceiling of human health
    • It's crazy how little we understand about mental health
    • It's only slightly less crazy how little we understand about physical health
    • Longevity research is good, but maximizing QALYs should be the goal
